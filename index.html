Asphalt Legends Unite (formerly known as Asphalt 9 Legends) is a racing simulator game made by developer Gameloft. It features a wide variety of cars from manufacturers all over the world. The automotive brands featured in this game range from the more common everyday brands like Chevrolet, Ford, Nissan, Honda to Luxury and exotic brands such as Lamborghini, Pagani, Ferrari, and much more.
